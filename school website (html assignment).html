About Green Valley School

Founded in 1995, GVS blends academic rigor with human values. Our faculty mentor each learner’s path through choice-rich programs, global exchanges, and meaningful service.

Campus: 25-acre green smart campus with solar power, LEED-aligned classrooms, STEM & media labs, and athletics village.

Mission: To cultivate curious, compassionate, and confident graduates ready to lead with purpose.
